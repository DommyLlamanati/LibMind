Why We Keep Losing the Infrastructure War

Current progressive data lives in Silicon Valley servers, mined by platforms that sell our attention to the highest bidder. Our movements are effectively taxed by the tech monopolies we oppose.

The Capability Gap

Conservative infrastructure has weaponized psychographics and network analysis to erode democracy. Progressive organizing still relies on 20th-century tactics while the opposition deploys 21st-century precision.

The Sustainability Crisis

Campaigns end. Staff disbands. Data disappears. We rebuild from zero every two years while the Heritage Foundation and Federalist Society operate on 50-year timelines.

The Alternative

A Cooperative Model for Movement Intelligence

We're not building a "progressive Cambridge Analytica." We're building its ethical inverse: open infrastructure owned by the people it serves.

  • Federated Architecture: Personal data stays on devices; only collective insights flow upward. cryptographically secured.
  • Open Source Everything: Algorithms audited by academics, code available to all progressive movements globally.
  • Consent by Design: Radical transparency in data collection with community veto power over all targeting strategies.
  • Economic Democracy: Worker-owned cooperative structure prevents the extractive dynamics of traditional political tech.
